My goal is to give my clients the tools to find opportunities and use their strengths.

Private Coaching

Taking into account circumstances, opportunities, and the hopes, I act as a guide to my partners so they can find their unique solution for their personal and work issues.

INDIVIDUAL TOPICS, WHICH I OFTEN COACH:

  • feeling unnecessary and "not good enough",
  • workplace conflicts,
  • powerlessness in some situations,
  • undervalued self,
  • work/life balance,
  • communication and presentation skill development,
  • mother-in-law/father-in-law conflicts, daughter/son conflicts,
  • relationship problems,
  • belonging to a group, feeling of exclusion, insignificance,
  • the sense of uncertainty,
  • and all forms of these above.

My experience demonstrates that the weight of the problems we carry can be reduced or stopped in a supportive coaching relationship.

Who am I in the relationship?

I am a partner on the journey, an interviewer who asks questions, and if you need advice or guidance, a teacher. All of this depends on the client, what she/he needs in the here and now. I think of myself mostly as "thoughts control", who helps clients catch the swirling thoughts in your mind and supports you to direct the energy of those thoughts in the direction of your desired goal.

FOR ME, AS A COACH, THE GREATEST SUCCESS IS THE SUCCESS AND SATISFACTION OF MY PARTNERS.

It's an amazing experience for me to see how my clients find their own inner strengths. This leads them to discover the world's previously unknown dimensions. In the light of the results achieved, they are my real ideals.

IF YOU'RE READY, I WILL HELP YOU CHANGE!

Based on my experience, issue raised often from old, deeply rooted beliefs. Questioning and exploring the beliefs energize the progress. If the client is open to it, I also like to use SELF DEVELOPMENT methods.

When we examine our life situations

  • the stressful thoughts about life, about others and ourselves disappear,
  • we see the world, and ourselves in it, in a new way,
  • as a result, we live a happier life.

Practicing the learned method

THE AWARENESS LEVEL OF MY COACHING PARTNERS RISES. THE CHANGE WILL BE LASTING AND PROFOUND.

Individual clients of mine include: Directors (CEO, Managing Director, International Operating Director, CFO), IT Group Manager, Teachers, Executive Assistant, Asset manager, Financial Manager, Full-Time Mom, Student, Attorneys, Senior Manager, HR Consultant, Coach, Entrepreneur, Trader, Restauranteur, and more.

I work in person and also online (e.g. Skype). I have over 400 hours coaching experience.
